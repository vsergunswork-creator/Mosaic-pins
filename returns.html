Returns & Refunds Policy

You have the right to return your order within 14 days of receiving the item, in accordance with EU consumer protection laws.

To be eligible for a return:

  • The item must be unused
  • In original condition and packaging

To request a return, please contact us before sending the item back:
support@mosaipins.space

Refunds will be processed within 7 days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defect or an error on our part.
