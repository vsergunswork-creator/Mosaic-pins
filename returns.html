Returns & Refunds

Support for issues, returns (if accepted) and refunds
← Back to Shop

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If something is wrong, contact us and we’ll help.

Damaged / wrong item

If your order arrives damaged or you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

  • Include your order details and photos.
  • We will arrange replacement or refund where applicable.

Returns (if accepted)

Returns may be accepted in certain cases. Items must be unused and in original condition.

  • Return shipping costs are paid by the buyer (unless we made a mistake).
  • We recommend using a tracked service.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method. Bank processing time can take a few business days.

How to contact us

Email: support@mosaicpins.space
Please include: order/session ID (if available) and a short description.

Important We do not accept returns for custom or personalized items unless they arrive damaged or defective.
