Shipping

We ship mosaic pins worldwide. Below is a simple overview of processing time, delivery and tracking.

Processing time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. During peak periods it may take a little longer.

Handcrafted & carefully packed

Shipping & tracking

Shipping options and cost are calculated at checkout. Whenever possible we provide a tracking number.

  • Tracking updates may appear with a delay.
  • Delivery time depends on destination and carrier.

Delivery estimates

  • EU: usually 3–10 business days
  • UK: usually 5–12 business days
  • USA / Canada: usually 7–18 business days
  • Worldwide: usually 10–25 business days

These are estimates, not guarantees.

Customs & import taxes

International orders may be subject to customs fees, VAT or import taxes. These charges are the buyer’s responsibility.
